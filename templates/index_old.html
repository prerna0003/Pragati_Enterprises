Our Heritage

Founded in 1985, SilkRoute has been preserving the art of traditional Indian weaving while infusing contemporary designs that appeal to the modern woman.

Each of our sarees is meticulously crafted by master weavers who have passed down their skills through generations, ensuring every thread tells a story.

We work directly with artisans across India, providing fair wages and sustainable livelihood while promoting indigenous textile traditions.
